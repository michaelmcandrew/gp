Join the Green Party today


 

Become a Green Party member today and you'll be joining a community of people dedicated to creating social and environmental change.

We don't get donations from big corporations and unions like other parties - we rely on our members and supporters for funding - making us a truly independent voice in politics.

Join by monthly or annual Direct Debit

You can join the Green Party via monthly Direct Debit which is our preferred method, as it helps save on administration costs. Through this kind of committed support we can plan our campaigns – month by month – secure in the knowledge that they are funded.

Please consider giving an additional donation on top of your membership fee. £5, £10 or even £20 a month could help provide the vital support that makes our campaigns and direct action for social and environmental justice possible.

Students and Young Greens

Students of any age and Young People can join the Green Party for just £5 a year.

If you're under 30 you'll also automatically become a member of the Young Greens.

The Young Greens are making a real difference to politics, campaigning on issues of real relevance to young people - both within colleges and universities, and within our local Green Parties.

Whether it's calling for the abolition of tuition fees, protecting the NHS, or leading the fight against climate change, Young Greens are at the forefront of efforts to build a better world for our generation, and ones to come.
